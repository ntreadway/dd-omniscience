The Problem

The Human
Ceiling.

Manual due diligence is the hidden liability in every CRE deal. When a broker is managing multiple transactions, the human brain fatigues — and fatigued brains miss things that kill six-figure commissions.

"The deal looked clean. It wasn't. A zoning overlay nobody caught cost me the commission."

— Anonymous Newport Beach CRE Broker
Fatigue-Driven Errors
A broker reviewing a 400-page rent roll at hour eight of a workday is not operating at peak acuity. Critical anomalies — hidden lease clauses, rent abatements, tenant options — vanish in the noise.
Regulatory Lag
Orange County municipal data — zoning amendments, environmental notices, permit histories — is fragmented across dozens of portals. No single human can monitor it all, in real time, across every active deal.
Compounding Deal Risk
One overlooked environmental flag. One miscalculated cap rate. One unverified tenant estoppel. Any single error compounds — turning a clean close into a liability event, a renegotiation, or a dead deal.
